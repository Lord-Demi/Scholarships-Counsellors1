Exploring Scholarships: Unveiling the New American University Scholar Honors

Embarking on your academic journey at ASU is more than a mere step; it's a leap into the realm of possibilities. For outstanding transfer students and new international freshmen, the New American University Scholar honors stand as a testament to your potential, offering not just financial support, but an immersive environment dedicated to knowledge, exploration, and solving the world's most pressing challenges.

Beyond Scholarships: A Journey of Empowerment

Imagine a place where your pursuit of academic success is met with unwavering support. The New American University Scholar program goes beyond traditional scholarships. It's a pathway designed to inspire, empower, and propel you toward a future marked by meaningful contributions. With a monetary incentive as your compass, you'll navigate an environment that values learning, research, and the exchange of ideas, equipping you with the tools to tackle society's most complex issues.

Seamless Integration: Your ASU Adventure Begins

When you step into ASU, you're already on the path to the New American University Scholar honors. As an enrolled student, you're automatically in the running for this prestigious award. There's no need for a separate application process. However, to be considered, make sure the ASU Undergraduate Admissions Office receives all your international admission application materials—admission application, official transcripts, test scores, financial guarantee, and application fee—by the scholarship priority date of April 1.

Illuminating Eligibility

The New American University Scholar program welcomes individuals who meet specific criteria, designed to encapsulate dedication and promise:

  • Global Aspirations: ASU is a global community, ready to embrace international students as they shape their futures.
  • Demonstrated Need: Financial circumstances won't limit your journey; they'll propel it forward.
  • Academic Excellence: Your academic journey becomes a canvas of achievement.
  • Language Proficiency: Fluent English communication ensures your ideas resonate on a global scale.
  • Exceptional Performance: Remarkable test scores and GPA highlight your dedication to academic growth.
  • F-1 Visa Advantage: F-1 visa holders are uniquely poised to reap the benefits of this remarkable program.
  • Sponsorship Exclusion: Sponsored students, while valued, are not eligible for this particular honor.

Continuation Awaits

Your story is waiting to unfold, and the New American University Scholar honors are the chapters that amplify your narrative. Beyond scholarships, these honors lay the foundation for an enriching academic journey—a journey that challenges, inspires, and shapes the future you've always envisioned.
